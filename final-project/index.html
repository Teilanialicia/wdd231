Welcome to the Psychology Graduate Planner

A personal guide to navigating graduate school and career paths in psychology

About This Project

This website is a final project for my web development class — but it's also something much more personal. I'm using it to organize my research and thoughts as I prepare to apply to psychology graduate programs.

With ADHD, anxiety, and a deep love of psychology, I wanted a space where I could break everything down visually and calmly. This planner is for me — and maybe for others like me who are navigating similar questions.

My Goal

I'm especially interested in becoming a clinical psychologist who can do both therapy and assessments. I want to understand what the path looks like — especially since I'm coming from a non-psych undergrad background.
